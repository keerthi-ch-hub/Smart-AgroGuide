About Smart AgroGuide

Smart AgroGuide is an AI-powered agriculture support system designed to help farmers, students, and agricultural researchers with:

  • Plant disease detection using deep learning
  • AI Chatbot for farming queries
  • Weather updates & advisories
  • Crop recommendation using soil parameters

Our goal is to make modern agriculture simple, smart, and accessible.
